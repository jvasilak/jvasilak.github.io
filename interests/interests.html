Interests

Soccer

I started playing soccer when I was 4 years old. According to my parents, I used to hate it, and I ran over to them crying during every practice because I didn't want to play. Luckily, my attitude improved very quickly and I decided to keep playing. I continued playing recreationally until high school and played for my high school and a club team while I was in high school. Since graduating high school I kept playing casually and have no plans of stopping anytime soon.

Outside of playing, I also love following professional soccer in my free time. I am a Liverpool fan, and have been lucky enough to attend two Liverpool friendly matches. I was also able to attend all 4 matches in Chicago during the 2016 Copa America, where I saw Messi score a 30 minute hat trick against Panama, team USA defeat Costa Rica 4-0, and an intense match between Chile and Colombia in the semi-finals.

Although I did not get to see a game at the Camp Nou, I was able to visit the stadium when I was in Barcelona. The stadium and museum inside it are some of my favorite places I have seen while traveling.
