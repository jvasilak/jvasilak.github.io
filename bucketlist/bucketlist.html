My Bucket List
3 years ago I decided to create my first bucket list while I was bored in an airport. Since then I have added a lot more to my bucket list and have made some progress checking off some of the items on the list. There is a large section of my bucket list dedicated to the places I wish to travel to, which are included on the travels page of my website.

Items I Have Checked Off

Here is a list of all of the items on my bucket list that I have completed so far.
  • Build a PC
    • This was the first item I checked off my bucket list. I built a gaming PC in 2024 with an Nvidia RTX 4070 GPU and AMD Ryzen 7800 CPU.
  • Go Karting
    • I went karting for the first time in Charlotte. It was very fun and I definitely want to try it again.
  • See Another Concert
    • I checked this one off when I saw Leon Bridges in May 2025. I've been to several other concerts since then, including: Tate McRae, Big Sean, and Bad Suns.
  • See Notre Dame Play in the College Football Playoff
    • I saw them play IU at Notre Dame stadium
  • Go to the College Football National Championship Game
    • Went to see Notre Dame in the 2025 CFP final, unfortunately they lost this one :(
  • See Zara Larson in Concert
    • I saw her open in Vancouver as an opener for Tate McRae. I am also going to see her again in Seattle next year.
  • Create a Pokemon Living Dex
    • It took me 2 years to fully complete, but I have a living dex of all Pokemon released through generation 8 in Pokemon Home, which was the most recent generation out when I added this to my list.
    I also have all but 4 of the generation 9 Pokemon and plan to add them to my living dex as soon as I can.
