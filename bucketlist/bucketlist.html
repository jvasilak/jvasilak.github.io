My Bucket List
3 years ago I decided to create my first bucket list while I was bored in an airport. Since then I have added a lot more to my bucket list and have also managed to complete a few items on the list. There is a large section of my bucket list dedicated to the places I wish to travel to, which are included on the travels page of my website.

Items I Have Checked Off

    • This was the first item I checked off my bucket list
  • Go Karting
    • I went karting for the first time in Charlotte. I definitely want to try it again.
  • See Another Concert
    • I went to ACL in 2024 and saw many different artists
  • See Notre Dame Play in the College Football Playoff
    • I saw them play IU at Notre Dame stadium
  • Go to the College Football National Championship Game
    • Went to see Notre Dame in the 2025 CFP final, unfortunately they lost this one :(
  • See Zara Larson in Concert
    • I saw her open in Vancouver as an opener for Tate McRae. I am going to see her again in 2026.
  • Create a Pokemon Living Dex
    • It took me 2 years to fully complete, but I have a living dex of all Pokemon released through gen8 in Pokemon Home, which was the most recent generation out when I added this to my list.
    I also have all but 4 of the gen9 Pokemon and plan to add them to my living dex when I can.
